That Time i got reincarnated as a slime

“That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime,” also known as TenSura, is a light novel series written by Fuse and illustrated by Mitz Vah. The story follows Satoru Mikami, a salaryman who is reincarnated in a sword and sorcery world as a slime after being murdered. He then builds a nation of monsters. The series originally started as a web novel on Shōsetsuka ni Narō from 2013 to 2016 and was later published as light novels by Micro Magazine starting in 2014. As of October 2023, twenty-one volumes have been released. The anime adaptation, produced by 8-Bit, premiered on October 1, 2018, and ran for 24 episodes plus one special episode in its first season.
